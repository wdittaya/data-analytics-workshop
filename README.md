# data-analytics-workshop
Getting insights from Thailand Tourism data

## Data Visualization for Tourism Executives: Strategic Market Insights

**Workshop Objective:** In this session, you will turn real-world Thailand tourist arrival and expenditure data (2017–2024) into a visually impactful slide deck. Your task is to move beyond basic data display and apply the "3-Minute Story" framework to recommend actionable strategies for tourism executives.

---

### General Design Guidelines for All Slides

* **Remove Clutter:** Simplify your charts by eliminating borders, gridlines, and unnecessary data markers.
* **Direct Labeling:** Label countries/regions directly on your charts instead of using legends.
* **Strategic Color:** Use a single muted color (such as gray) for most data. Highlight your key insight with a contrasting, bright brand color (e.g., gold or deep blue).
* **Actionable Titles:** Write explanatory titles that highlight insights (e.g., "India Shows the Steepest Growth in Arrivals"), not generic data descriptions.

---

### Slide 1: Core Market Profile

* **Student Task:** Identify Thailand’s major tourist segments by analyzing incoming volume, total spending, and spend per head.
* **Audience:** Executives (TAT Board / Strategic Planning)
* **Format:** Workshop Slide
* **Visualization Instructions:** Create a Scatter Plot displaying three dimensions: X-axis (Number of Incoming Tourists), Y-axis (Spending per Head), and bubble size (Total Spending). Add a quadrant overlay for easy grouping, and directly label the top 5-7 countries.

### Slide 2: Growth & Momentum

* **Student Task:** Analyze which countries are showing an upward trend in visits to Thailand.
* **Audience:** Executives (Marketing & Promotions)
* **Format:** Workshop Slide
* **Visualization Instructions:** Use a Line Graph to show trends over time. Group stagnant or declining countries into one light-gray “Average/Other” line, and highlight 1–2 countries with the sharpest growth post-2021. Label the start and end points only.

### Slide 3: Untapped Goldmine

* **Student Task:** Determine which countries have high spending per visitor but low arrival numbers, and could be targeted for promotion.
* **Audience:** Executives (Budget Allocation & Advertising)
* **Format:** Workshop Slide
* **Visualization Instructions:** Exclude countries with over 1 million visitors, and rank the rest by “Spending per Head”. Use a Horizontal Bar Chart of the top 10, highlighting the top 3 in a bright accent color. Instead of an X-axis, place the spending value at the end of each bar.

### Slide 4: Strategic Anomalies

* **Student Task:** Investigate and present any interesting or unexpected patterns you find in the data.
* **Audience:** Executives (TAT Leadership)
* **Format:** Workshop Slide
